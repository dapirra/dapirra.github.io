Flashcards App

View on Github

About:

I collaborated with a group of students to create this app when I was a student at St. Joseph's College in an Android Development class.

Description:

This app allows you to create flashcards quickly and easily. Customize your own decks of flashcards and get valuable study time while creating them. Save your decks for those end-of-semester finals and study with shuffle mode. With added Wear OS support you can now study right from your wrist!

Features:

  • Has a shuffle mode so that you can test yourself.
  • Favorite flashcards that you have difficulty with.
  • Flashcards can be synced to your android watch.
  • Is capable of rearranging flashcards in any order you like.
